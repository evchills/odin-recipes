Creamy Italian Tortellini Soup

Warm pasta pockets, a healthy dose of veggies, and a broth that you could drink in a glass!

"This soup is the perfect comfort food for days when you need it! Evan and I will always go back for seconds (or thirds)!"

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp. olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion (diced)
  • 1 celery stalk (diced)
  • 1 carrot (diced)
  • 3 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 2 tsp. dried Italian herb seasoning
  • 1 (28 oz.) can of Tuttorosso diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
  • 5 cups of vegetable broth
  • 9-10 oz. cheese tortellini
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot on medium-low heat. Add carrot, celery, and onion. Once soft, add garlic, Italian herbs, and red pepper flakes if you want it spicy.
  2. Stir in tomatoes, garlic powder, vegetable broth, salt and pepper. Cover pot and increase heat to medium. Once the broth comes to a boil, simmer until carrots are fork tender (10-15 minutes).
  3. Add cheese tortellini and cook for a little less time than the package indicates (1-2 minutes).
  4. Reduce heat to low and add heavy cream and spinach. Let simmer until the spinach has wilted (2-3 minutes). Serve with parmesan and fresh basil.
