Buffalo Cauliflower Tacos

You'll never order wings again!

"Oh man... this one is good! Crispy buffalo slathered cauliflower in a warm taco with all the toppings. We usually eat these at the kitchen counter because we can't make it to the table!"

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup buffalo sauce
  • 1 avocado (sliced)
  • 1 head of cauliflower (chopped)
  • bunch of fresh cilantro (chopped roughly)
  • 1 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1 head of romaine lettuce (chopped roughly)
  • 1 tsp. chili powder
  • 1 tsp. pepper
  • 4 tsp. olive oil
  • 8 taco-sized flour tortillas
  • 1 jalapeno for topping (optional)
  • green onion for topping (optional)
  • ranch dressing for topping (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees and line a baking sheet with foil.
  2. Mix the cauliflower, olive oil, garlic powder, chili powder, pepper, and buffalo sauce together in a bowl. Add to the baking sheet and roast for about 20 minutes.
  3. Remove from the oven and toss in a little more buffalo sauce.
  4. Warm the tortillas in the microwave covered with a damp paper towl 30-40 seconds if desired.
  5. Add the remaining ingredients on as toppings and enjoy!
