White Bean Chicken Chili

This dish brings the Southwest right to your home!

"This rich chili is packed with flavor and nutrition! With great options for toppings, this hearty meal is sure to satisfy even the heaviest of appetites."

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp. olive oil
  • 1 1/2 lbs of chicken breast (cubed)
  • 1 onion (diced)
  • 5 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp. jalapeno (diced)
  • 1 cup cilantro (chopped roughly)
  • 1 tsp. cumin
  • 1 tsp. oregano
  • 1 tsp. paprika
  • 1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 tsp. salt and pepper
  • 4 (15.8 oz.) cans of white beans (we use Cannellini and Great Northern)
  • 6 cups of chicken broth
  • 16 oz. frozen corn
  • 2 (4 oz.) cans of diced green chiles
  • plain Greek yogurt for topping (optional)
  • oyster crackers for topping (optional)
  • shredded mozzarella cheese for topping (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Add cubed chicken and saute chicken for 3-5 minutes until just cooked through. Seat aside.
  2. Add more oil and diced onions, garlic, and jalapeno. Cook until softened. Add 1/2 the cilantro, cumin, oregano, paprika, cayenne, and the salt and pepper and cook over medium heat for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add 4 cans of drained and rinsed white beans and the broth. Let it simmer for at least 30 minutes. Once the beans have softened, use a potato masher to mash the beans.
  4. Add the chicken, frozen corn, and diced green chiles. Cook for another 10 minutes then stir in the rest of the cilantro.
  5. Top with whatever is left and serve!
